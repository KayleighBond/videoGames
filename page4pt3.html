Realm Royale

Game Description

Realm Royale is a free-to-play third-person shooter battle royale game developed by Heroic Leap Games. The game features multiple character classes each with unique abilities. It is a spin-off of the hero shooter Paladins, where it originated as a game mode known as Paladins: Battlegrounds.

My Personal Opinions And Thoughts On The Game

I really enjoyed playing Realm Royale. When it came out I hadn't seen any games like it, it was definitely its own game. Its alot different from other games also.In the beginning you choose what class you would like to play such as warrior, Mage, Engineer, or assassin. You open chests to find weapons, heath and shield potions, and abilities. You could destroy what you or your teammates do not want including armor. When you destroy the objects it will in turn give you shards and you may use them at a forge. You can buy health and shield potions, armor, a random legendary weapon, A random class ability, or a random class weapon. You can only carry 200 shards at a time. Your teammates can resurrect you buy spending shards at the forge. While this being said, when you are "downed" you turn into a chicken, your teammates can not revive you. They must protect you until the timer ends and then you will be back into the game. For the most part this game is very fun to me, and I really enjoyed playing it.

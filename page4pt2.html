Spellbreak

Game Description

Spellbreak is an epic fantasy action spellcasting game where players choose a class, weave spectacular spell combinations and fight other players to become an all-powerful battlemage. ... Play with your friends and enemies in multiple game modes, including Battle Royale and Clash.

My Personal Opinions And Thoughts On The Game

I found out about this game when some of my old friends messaged me and told me I should download it and play with them because it is a good game that I would enjoy and also get mad at (They like to make me mad on purpose). The game is really good and still in the beta form. So far there is a solo and trio option and they are working on duos. I would really like to see the game become a 4 player game. With having only 42 players in each lobby it sure does get crazy. There is almost always someone to fight. The whole game is wizards casting spells in order to elimate the others. Something that I found out the hard way is that team killing is on as of right now.

Friday The 13Th

Game Description

The entire focus of Friday the 13th: The Game is multiplayer. Survival is entirely up to you, the player, as you either stealthily hide from Jason or work together as a team to escape or bring the fight to Jason. Playing as a counselor is all about risk and reward, giving players multiple means of triumph over Jason!

My Personal Opinions And Thoughts On The Game

It is not my first choice as a game to play. But it is not the worst, it is super fun if you have a group of friends to play with. It is easy to learn how to play. And I personally really like the back story. I think I have played this like a total of 5 times.

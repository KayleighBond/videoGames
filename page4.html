Game Description

Save the World is a player-versus-environment cooperative game, with four players collaborating towards a common objective on various missions. ... When they land, they must scavenge for weapons, items, resources, and even vehicles while trying to stay alive and to attack and eliminate other players.

This video game has two completely different parts to it. The first part of the game is Save the World which is where you build to defend whatever it is that you are trying to protect during that mission. And then you fight off a bunch of waves of zombies and the better you are the higher up you are in the game, the more difficult it gets to kill the zombies and they fight back. You also collect a bunch of different types of resources in order to build the weapon blueprints that you collect. You can also build traps and other sorts of stuff to help you defend and kill all the zombies.

The second part of the game is the Battle Royale portion. When it came out is when the game began to get all of its hype. It is a multiplayer game which you jump out of a battle bus and fly to where you would like to begin your match. From there you find weapons and other things such a boogie bombs and cars for mobility to get around the map. You eliminate all the other people in the lobby to earn the Victory Royale. If you had gotten eliminated you could just reque and go again. Customization of oyur character with the things you have earned is an option. There is also an 100 tier battle pass, you can get game currency, skins, weapon skins, and much more from just playing the game.

My Personal Opinions And Thoughts On The Game

When this game first came out I absolutely loved it, well the Battle Royale part. I was very bad but I kept playing every single day after school until atleast 1 in the morning. There was a point in time when if someone put up a ramp and wall, you knew they were a good player and you should just leave the game. Now you see people building to sky height during a battle. Once people started to become good at the game, you had to progress quickly in order to keep up and even have a chance to win a match. The first 3 seasons were my favorite. They were chill and you did not have to sweat just to play a game. I continued to play a rediculous amount up until chapter 2. I just quit after chapter 2 began because in my opinion they have ruined the game and it is not even fun anymore. I have spent so much money on my account, I would say it is probably close to 4 or 5 hundred dollars. And I dont really have much to show for it either, besides well over 100 skins.

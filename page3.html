Sea Of Thieves

Game Description

Sea of Thieves is a first-person multiplayer game in which players cooperate with each other to explore an open world via a pirate ship. ... The game features a progression system that only unlocks cosmetic items as the development team wanted to encourage both casual and experienced players to play together.

My Personal Opinions And Thoughts On The Game

This game is second on my favorite game list. It is such a fun game to be able to just chill with your friends and play. You get your pirate ship and get your crew together. You can vote on what kind of task you would like to do and then sail the seas. There are several diffent tasks you could chose from, my favorite is the ghost ship one. You just sink all the ghost ships that you see. I also really enjoy the riddle ones, but they can be really difficult. They are very time consuming and after a few hours of trying to figure them out I usually just end up looking up the answer online.
